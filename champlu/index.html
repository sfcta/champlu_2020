San Francisco Land Use Allocation 2021 (LUA 2021)

  • This LUA 2021 allocates the Bay Area Metro regional projections of households and jobs for San Francsico from the Plan Bay Area 2050 (adopted October 2021) to the County Transportation Authority's 981 sub-city traffic analysis zones (TAZs) for use in their transportation model (SF-CHAMP).
  • It also becomes SF Planning's base long range land use forecast for San Francisco, consistent with .

How to use this map

  • Select a metric (households or jobs by sector) to display it on the map for all TAZs.
  • Move both ends of the year slider to a single value to map selected metric for that particular year.
  • Move year slider ends to different years, to the map the difference in metric values between the two years.
  • Click on a TAZ on the map to see projected TAZ-specific metric for all years on the chart.
